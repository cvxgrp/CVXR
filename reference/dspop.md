# Direct Standardization: Population

Randomly generated data for direct standardization example. Sex was
drawn from a Bernoulli distribution, and age was drawn from a uniform
distribution on \\10,\ldots,60\\. The response was drawn from a normal
distribution with a mean that depends on sex and age, and a variance of
1.

## Usage

``` r
dspop
```

## Format

A data frame with 1000 rows and 3 variables:

- y:

  Response variable

- sex:

  Sex of individual, coded male (0) and female (1)

- age:

  Age of individual
